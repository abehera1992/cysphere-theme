Solutions

Cyber sphere is a total IT solutions and services provider, supporting its esteemed clients across North America and Asia. We expend considerable resources in understanding our clients and their business needs, before embarking on designing and developing customized technical solutions. We strongly believe in quality in its various connotations - quality of solutions, service, support and relationships.

Having been established as a staff augmentation company, in 2004, in Nashua, New Hampshire, USA, the organization has morphed into a total IT solutions and services provider. It has acquired strong presence with offices in the USA, and India. Our Professionals offer more than 20 years of experience providing IT Consulting, Consult to Hire, Direct Hire and Project Based solutions, with a diverse range of clients from small niche organizations to mid-size and Fortune 1000 firms.
